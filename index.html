CardSwap

A game by EpicPuppy613

2-5 min
2-8 players
Ages 7+

CardSwap is a quick card drafting game. Players will choose a card out of their hand and then pass the hand to the player on their right.
Scoring these cards will be different for the amount of each of the cards you have.
This game is ment to be played as a fun family game during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Materials

4 Reference cards
Score sheet
Standard 52 deck of cards (No Jokers)
Links: Reference Cards Score Sheets

Setup

1. Take the 4 reference cards and lay them around the table where everyone can see at least one.
2. Shuffle the deck of cards.
3. According to the table below, deal cards to each player.
4. Set aside the rest of the cards.

Players Cards
2 15
3 12
4 10
5 9
6 8
7 7
8 6

Gameplay

During each round, players will choose a card out of their hand and place it down in front of them, face down.
Then, they will take the rest of the cards and pass it to the player on their right.
Play repeats until all of the cards have been chosen and kept.
There is no need for a starting player as all actions are taken simultaneously.

Scoring

After all cards have been chosen, scoring is as follows:
1 of a card: score card's value
2 of a card: score 20 minus the card's value
3 of a card: score 4 times the card's value
4 of a card: score 50 minus 2 times the card's value

Example Scoring

Hand: A,A,A,A,2,2,3,3,7,J,Q,Q,K,K,K (for 2 player game)

2: 18 pts (20-2)

8: None
9: None
10: None
J-11: 11 pts (1x11)
Q-12: 8 pts (20-12)
K-13: 52 pts (4x13)

Total Score: 161 pts

The winner of the game is the player with the most points.
